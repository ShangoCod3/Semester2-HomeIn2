Science

  • I’ve taken part in programs at the Rockville Science Center . Most recently, I completed a class where we learned the basics of Java and created a working cash register project.
  • I’ve also done science classes through Outschool, an online platform that offers live classes on a wide variety of science topics.
  • At home, I did a fun experiment using an egg and vinegar to explore chemical reactions. We used the scientific method to make predictions, observe how the eggshell dissolved due to the acetic acid in vinegar, and explain the reaction between calcium carbonate and acid. We also beet juice as coloring in order to observe Osmosis.
